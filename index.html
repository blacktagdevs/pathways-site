Pathways Scholarship

"Each Journey Starts with a Step"

We believe in fostering creative thinkers and innovators who positively impact their communities. The Pathways Scholarship supports Westover High School seniors pursuing STEM or Creative Arts with five one-time awards of $1,000 each.

What We Value

We seek students who demonstrate these core qualities in their academic journey and community involvement.

D

Direction

Clear ambitions and a purposeful path toward your chosen field. We want to see that you know where you're headed.

I

Initiative

Proactive leadership in academics, extracurriculars, or community projects. Show us you take action without being asked.

C

Community Involvement

Demonstrated service and leadership within your community. We value those who give back and lift others up.
