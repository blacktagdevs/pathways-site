Quick Answers

Here are answers to questions we receive most often.

First, submit an Application of Interest between January 12 and February 12. Then, complete your full application (including your 750-word essay) by late March. See our timeline for complete details.

A minimum GPA of 3.0 is required to be eligible for the Pathways Scholarship. This requirement reflects our commitment to supporting students who have demonstrated academic dedication. If your GPA is slightly below 3.0, we encourage you to focus on your studies and apply for other scholarship opportunities.

You don't need to have declared a specific major, but you should have a clear direction toward a STEM or Creative Arts pathway. In your essay, share your interests, what fields you're considering, and why these areas appeal to you. We value students who are thoughtfully exploring their options.
